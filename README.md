# Preparing for Influenza Season: Predicting Staffing Needs Across U.S. States

![Logo](Logo.PNG)

## Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the CareerFoundry Data Analytics Immersion program.

The objective was to support a medical staffing agency in forecasting staffing needs during influenza season across U.S. states by analyzing historical flu trends, demographics, mortality rates, and vaccination patterns.

The project explores how integrated public health datasets can support predictive workforce planning and healthcare resource allocation.

---

## Key Research Questions

- Which states have experienced the highest influenza-related hospitalizations and deaths?
- When does influenza season typically begin and peak across regions?
- How do vaccination rates influence staffing requirements and flu severity?
- Which demographic groups are most vulnerable during influenza outbreaks?
